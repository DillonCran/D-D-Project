Steps for a believable Universe

Layed out in concepts should be whether if your world is SCIFI or fantasy

FOr this tutorial it will mostly be focused on a sigular world, which is more inclined to fantasy, but can be SCIFI too

You should start with a general creation story, the people in your world can know this but dont need to.

IRL example (big bang -> expansion -> cooling)(God creating earth in 7 days)

These are not necissary for a world to be believable, but adding these will help with worldbuilding later

Pick a general shape/colour for your galaxy. Life is most likely to form on the outskirts of the galaxy

IRL example (Milky way is a spiral galaxy, massive black hole at centre, with a blueish tint)
