Welcome to the beginning!

Often when people are starting a new worldbuilding project, theyre not sure where or how to begin.

THis is where this website comes in handy! Outlining each step in order form the most basic to complex ideas.

Ideally in the future I would like to impliment an AI assistant that will help generate some of the more complicated parts, but everything is designed to be fully accessable without assistance.

Often worldbuilding specific niches that you are interested in requires outside knowledge beyond tyhe scope of this website, so I will try and link wikipedia articles when applicable.
